## Channel and Geo

- Web sessions correlation with revenue: **0.3211** (moderate positive signal).

## Promotion Economics
- Promo line share: **38.66%**, but promo net-revenue share is only **29.88%**.
- Promo AOV: **16,958** vs non-promo AOV **25,083**.
- Estimated margin with promo: **-14.46%** vs non-promo **19.96%**.

## Operations
- Highest cancellation volume payment method: **credit_card** (28,452 cancelled orders).
- Highest cancellation rate payment method: **cod** (16.00%).
- Top return reason: **wrong_size** (34.97% of return records).
- Delivery lead-time median/p90/p95: **4.0/7.0/7.0 days**.

## Customer Lifecycle
- Repeat <= 180 days: **27.88%**.
- Repeat <= 365 days: **42.62%**.
- Highest orders-per-customer age group: **55+** (7.27).

## Priority Business Actions
1. **Tighten discount governance**: cap high-discount campaigns and prioritize channels with better value retention.
2. **Geo-focused inventory allocation**: protect East availability while reducing overstock in lower-yield periods.
3. **Payment and checkout optimization**: reduce cancellation leakage for high-risk payment paths.
4. **Returns prevention**: focus on top return reasons and high-return categories/sizes with fit and quality interventions.
5. **Lifecycle orchestration**: trigger re-engagement before 180-day churn windows for high-frequency cohorts.
